Nasi Lemak Recipe

Description

Nasi Lemak is a famous Malaysian food. It is Sweet, Savoury and Spicy!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Coconut Milk
  • Chilli Paste (Sambal)
  • Ginger
  • Shallot
  • Anchovies
  • Eggs
  • Peanuts

Steps

  1. Wash and prepare rice with coconut milk.
  2. Add sliced ginger and sliced shallots to rice and coconut milk mix.
  3. Cook chilli paste until it is fully cooked (wait until oil appears on top of the chilli paste).
  4. Fry Anchovies, Eggs and Peanuts
  5. Add cooked coconut rice, sambal and fried sides!
