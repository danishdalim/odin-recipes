Char Kuey Teow Recipe

Description

Char Kuey Teow is one of the most famous dishes in Penang. Made from combination of sauces along with noodles and prawns!

Ingredients

  • Kuey Teow
  • Maggi's Chilli Sauce
  • Beansprout
  • Chives
  • Prawn
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Wash kuey teow and ingredients
  2. Cook prawn with oil on high heat
  3. Add 1-2 tablespoons of Maggi's chilli sauce
  4. Add kuey teow and cook until soft
  5. Add egg and stir until done
